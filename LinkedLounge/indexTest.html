Our Mission

In 644, Ferneticus Brimsocket launched “The Linked Lounge” from his kitchen as a weekly gathering for like-minded entrepreneurs to meet one another and grow their brands. It’s grown a lot since then. We operate from a place of curiosity and nonjudgment, and we start hard conversations, crack open taboos, and look for profitable connection and resonance everywhere we can find it. We don’t mind being the tip of the spear—in short, we go first so you don’t have to. We’re glad you’re here.
